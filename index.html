FAQ

Do I need to rewrite my whole pipeline?

No. Most fixes are additive – you keep your existing workflow and add a conda-lock file and a pinned base image. Most pipelines are production-ready in under 4 hours of work.

Which Nextflow versions does this cover?

DSL2 (v21+) with Conda, Docker, or Singularity profiles. The checklist covers both local development and HPC cluster environments.

Is this suitable for published / peer-reviewed analyses?

Yes – the audit specifically checks for the reproducibility requirements of journals like Nature Methods, Genome Biology, and eLife. It also covers FAIR data principles.

What if I use Singularity instead of Conda?

The toolkit covers Singularity, Docker, and Conda. The checklist flags which sections are relevant to your specific setup.
